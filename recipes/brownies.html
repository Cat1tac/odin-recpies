How to make Brownies

Ingredients

  • Brownie Mix
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 tbps of water
  • 2/3 cup of vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Put all ingredients into a bowl and thoroughly mix them together
  3. Pour brownie mix into a pan and put into oven for 23-27 minutes
  4. After brownies are done, let sit for 5 minutes
  5. Cut and Enjoy!
